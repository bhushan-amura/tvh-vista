specification

Structure

RCC framed structure.


Wall

RCC structure with Shear wall. Ceiling and inner walls with patty finish. Outer walls with neat finish. Emulsion paint for inner walls.


Flooring

Imported branded 18′X18′ ceramic tiles. Optional wooden flooring in the master bedroom. Granite flooring in all lifts and lobbies. Kota stone for staircase and common areas.


Main door

Main doors with teak wood panel and varnish finish. Other doors – country wood frame hardcore flush doors with enamel paint finish.


Window Frames

Aluminium window frames. Aluminium shutters with sliding frame and 4 mm float clear glasses MS grill for safety in windows.


Kitchen

Granite cooking platform of 18 sq. ft. with ceramic tile dadoing up to 2 ft. ht. One single bowl SS sink with drain board. Separate tap provision for Panchayat and bore well water.


Toilet

Ceramic tile dadoing up to 7 ft. ht. Imported CP and ceramic fittings.


Power back up

Backup power for common amenities such as lift, pumps, common lights, etc. and inverter power for all lights and fans inside the flat except the 15/20 amps points.


Power Supply

Three phase power supply with concealed wiring and modular, ISI certified switches.
